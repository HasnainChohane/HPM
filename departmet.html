About MediCare Hospital

At MediCare Hospital, your health is our highest priority. We are a modern, patient-focused medical center offering advanced healthcare services with compassion, commitment, and clinical excellence.

Our hospital is home to highly skilled doctors, nurses, and specialists who work around the clock to provide ethical, personalized treatment. From preventive care to emergency services—we're here for you.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, accessible, and compassionate healthcare that improves lives.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare provider, known for excellence, innovation, and patient satisfaction.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: Caring with empathy and respect.
  • Integrity: Upholding ethics and professionalism.
  • Excellence: Highest standards in care.
  • Innovation: Embracing new technology.
  • Teamwork: Collaborating for better outcomes.
  • Accountability: Responsible and transparent actions.

  • Immunizations & vaccinations

  • Developmental delay assessments

Child Development & Therapy Center

Support for children with learning or developmental challenges.

  • ADHD / Autism support programmes

Cardiology Department

Comprehensive heart care—from diagnosis to recovery—with advanced cardiology technology.

Our Cardiology Services

  • ECG, stress testing & cardiac imaging
  • Angiography & angioplasty
  • Heart rhythm monitoring
  • Cardiac rehabilitation programs
  • Blood pressure & cholesterol care
